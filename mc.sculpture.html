Pokemon (wiki)
Pikachu Ditto Ditto-Chu Within Minecraft you are given the ability to stack blocks and form pixel art. For my project I was brought into a private server and presented with large quantities of different color blocks. I then used the blocks to make a sort of museum sculpture where a player is able to climb to the top while being informed with random facts about "Ditto, and Ditto-Chu"

Artist Statement

|P3.Minecraft.Sculpture|

|A surprise visit from Ditto-Chu| It is the form in which a unique ditto attempts to transform into a pikachu by memory. On this sculpture you'll find more facts about ditto (to which there are also hidden facts scattered about the inside and outside of the sculpture) as you climb to the top. This sculpture, to me, reminds me of being something you aren't, until you embody it. The idea is not fully realized until you fully realize it and actualize it. And that on its own is what makes each person unique.

Artist Statement
|Enjoy ALL|

There is a fine line on what the "norm" is (in the US) for what types of specific animals we commonly eat. But for people coming from different cultures or countries... it may seem limiting or this idea of limiting your pallet may seem foreign. Once you say no to new experiences (in this case different types of meat) you limit your pallet of texture, consistency and preparation. This series includes that fuzziness.

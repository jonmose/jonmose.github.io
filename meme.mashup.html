How it was made.
This series is composed of essential photoshopping.

Below is my thought process and how the spongebob/minion meme was generally composed.
spongebob memes (research)
minion memes (research) what others are doing (research) main spongebob (image source) main minion (image source)
In order to fully interpret the two memes I began my research through platforms like knowyourmeme and even pintrest
From there I looked for my main source images I wanted to blend together along with the original "meme-tagline" to fully embody the idea behind this meme mashup. When loading up the images into a single photoshop document I start by making a selection on the elements of each image that I want and seperate them by cloning the same image (thus making more layers with movable parts while having copies of the original image untouched or edited). From there it's generally just moving the different elements around and adding text.
